Melanie Martinez

Melanie Martinez is a muscial artist. She encorporates unique arrangements to her music. She doesn't follow what
others expect from her. With her album K-12 she created a visual album that was stunning. Everything from her
style, to music, to her performance work is spectacular.

Tati Moon

Tati Moon is a disabled digital artist that is from Germany. Her work reminds me a lot of the type of drawings
I did when I was younger (except her work is far better.) I found her during the 2020 pandemic lockdown and
she inspired me to begin drawing again. I love her use of color which is something I lack in my own art style.

Rikki-Kay Middleton

I draw a lot of inspiration for my own work from tattoos, especially because I typically work in black and white.
Rikki-Kay is a tattoo artist. I love her techniques for shading. She combines shading, stippling and sketching to create
a unique style of work.
